Company Overview

Swiss Metal Trading is a leading metal commodity trading company based in Geneva, Switzerland. We specialize in sourcing high-quality metals from producing regions, primarily in Africa and South America, and distributing them to markets across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

With decades of industry experience, we have established a reputation for reliability, integrity, and excellence in the global metal commodities market. Our expertise spans the entire supply chain, from origination to distribution, ensuring seamless operations and consistent quality for our clients worldwide.
