Our Operations

Discover how we connect metal-producing regions with global markets through our integrated business model and extensive distribution network.

Business Model Overview

Swiss Metal Trading operates on a comprehensive business model that spans the entire metal commodities value chain. Our core operations involve originating high-quality metals from producing regions, primarily in Africa and South America, and distributing them to industrial markets across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

Our integrated approach allows us to maintain quality control throughout the supply chain while optimizing logistics and ensuring timely delivery to our clients. By maintaining direct relationships with producers and end-users, we create value through efficient market access, risk management, and supply chain optimization.

The foundation of our business model is built on:

  • Strategic sourcing partnerships in metal-producing regions
  • Efficient logistics and transportation networks
  • Deep market knowledge and trading expertise
  • Strong relationships with industrial clients worldwide
  • Robust risk management and quality control systems

Metal Origination

Our origination operations focus on sourcing high-quality metal commodities from key producing regions, primarily in Africa and South America. We work directly with miners, processors, and local partners to secure reliable supply while ensuring adherence to quality standards and responsible sourcing practices.

Africa

In Africa, we maintain strategic partnerships across copper-rich regions in the Democratic Republic of Congo and Zambia, as well as bauxite operations in Guinea and manganese producers in South Africa and Ghana. Our local teams work closely with suppliers to ensure quality control and regulatory compliance.

South America

Our South American operations center on copper sourcing from Chile and Peru, zinc from Bolivia, and aluminum from Brazil. We have established long-term relationships with major producers and maintain regional offices to facilitate direct engagement with our supply partners.

Global Distribution Network

Our extensive distribution network spans four continents, allowing us to efficiently deliver metal commodities to industrial clients worldwide. From our strategic hub in Geneva, we coordinate global logistics and maintain regional distribution centers to ensure timely delivery and responsive service.

North America

Our North American distribution operations serve manufacturing clients across the United States and Canada, with logistics hubs in Chicago and Houston facilitating efficient delivery throughout the region. We supply metal commodities to automotive manufacturers, construction companies, and industrial equipment producers.

Europe

From our headquarters in Geneva, we coordinate European distribution to industrial clients across the continent. Our European network includes warehousing facilities in Rotterdam and Hamburg, serving manufacturing centers in Germany, France, Italy, and Eastern Europe.

Middle East

Our Middle Eastern operations focus on supplying metal commodities to the region's growing industrial base and construction sector. With a regional office in Dubai, we serve clients across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and other Gulf states.

Asia

Our Asian distribution network serves the region's dynamic manufacturing sectors, with particular focus on China, Japan, South Korea, and Southeast Asia. We maintain offices in Singapore and Shanghai to coordinate logistics and client relationships across this diverse and rapidly growing market.

Metals & Commodities

Swiss Metal Trading specializes in a diverse portfolio of metal commodities, focusing on base metals that are essential to global industrial production. Our expertise spans the following key metal categories:

Copper

Essential for electrical applications, construction, and manufacturing. We source high-grade copper primarily from Chile, Peru, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.

Aluminum

Valued for its lightweight properties and used extensively in transportation, packaging, and construction. Our aluminum is sourced from operations in Guinea, Brazil, and other producing regions.

Zinc

Critical for galvanizing steel and manufacturing brass. We supply zinc from mines in Bolivia, Peru, and other key producing countries.

Nickel

Essential for stainless steel production and battery technology. Our nickel is sourced from operations in Indonesia, the Philippines, and other producing regions.

Operational Processes & Logistics

Our operational processes are designed to ensure efficiency, reliability, and quality control throughout the metal commodities supply chain. From mine to market, we implement rigorous procedures to maintain the integrity of our products and the effectiveness of our services.

Quality Control

We implement comprehensive quality control measures at every stage of the supply chain. Our technical teams conduct regular inspections and testing to ensure all metal commodities meet industry specifications and client requirements. This includes:

  • Pre-shipment inspections at origin
  • Laboratory testing for composition and purity
  • Certification verification and documentation
  • Quality audits at warehousing facilities

Logistics Management

Our logistics operations are optimized to ensure efficient transportation and timely delivery of metal commodities across global supply chains. We coordinate:

  • Inland transportation from mines to ports
  • Ocean freight and shipping arrangements
  • Customs clearance and documentation
  • Warehousing and inventory management
  • Last-mile delivery to client facilities

Risk Management

We employ sophisticated risk management strategies to mitigate market, operational, and financial risks. Our approach includes:

  • Hedging strategies to manage price volatility
  • Diversified sourcing to ensure supply reliability
  • Insurance coverage for cargo and operations
  • Compliance monitoring and regulatory tracking
  • Counterparty risk assessment

Technology Integration

We leverage advanced technology to enhance operational efficiency and provide real-time visibility across our supply chain:

  • Supply chain tracking and monitoring systems
  • Digital documentation and transaction processing
  • Market intelligence and analytics platforms
  • Inventory management systems
  • Client portal for order tracking and management
