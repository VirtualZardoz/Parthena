Metal Origination Regions

Our metal origination operations focus on key producing regions in Africa and South America, where we work directly with miners, processors, and local partners to secure high-quality metal commodities while ensuring responsible sourcing practices.

Africa

Africa represents a critical sourcing region for Swiss Metal Trading, with rich deposits of copper, cobalt, bauxite, manganese, and other valuable metals. Our African operations are centered on:

Democratic Republic of Congo

A major source of copper and cobalt, where we maintain strategic partnerships with established mining operations. Our local team ensures compliance with responsible sourcing standards and quality control protocols.

Zambia

An important copper-producing country where we work with both large-scale mining companies and medium-sized operations to secure high-grade copper for global markets.

Guinea

Home to some of the world's largest bauxite reserves, where we source raw materials for aluminum production through established partnerships with mining companies.

South Africa

A diverse mining jurisdiction where we source manganese, chrome, and other metals through long-term supply agreements with leading producers.

Ghana

An emerging source for various metals, where we are developing new partnerships to expand our sourcing portfolio.

South America

South America is a cornerstone of our sourcing strategy, with its rich mineral resources and established mining infrastructure. Our South American operations focus on:

Chile

The world's largest copper producer, where we maintain strategic relationships with major mining companies to secure premium copper cathodes and concentrates for global distribution.

Peru

A significant producer of copper, zinc, and other metals, where our local team works with established mining operations to source high-quality products that meet international standards.

Bolivia

An important source of zinc and other metals, where we have developed relationships with key producers to secure reliable supply for our distribution network.

Brazil

A diverse mining jurisdiction where we source bauxite for aluminum production and other metal commodities through established partnerships.

Global Distribution Networks

From our strategic hub in Geneva, Switzerland, we coordinate the distribution of metal commodities to markets worldwide. Our extensive distribution network spans North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia, ensuring efficient delivery and responsive service to our clients.

North America

Our North American distribution network serves manufacturing clients across the United States and Canada, with a focus on:

  • Key Markets: Automotive manufacturing, construction, electronics, aerospace
  • Distribution Hubs: Chicago, Houston
  • Logistics Infrastructure: Rail connections, port facilities, warehousing
  • Client Base: Major manufacturers, metal service centers, industrial conglomerates

Through our North American operations, we provide reliable supply of copper, aluminum, zinc, nickel, and other metals to support the region's diverse manufacturing base.

Europe

From our headquarters in Geneva, Switzerland, we coordinate European distribution to industrial clients across the continent:

  • Key Markets: Automotive, engineering, construction, electronics
  • Distribution Hubs: Rotterdam, Hamburg
  • Logistics Infrastructure: Port facilities, rail connections, warehousing
  • Client Base: Manufacturing companies, metal processors, industrial groups

Our European network serves clients in Germany, France, Italy, Spain, and Eastern Europe, providing a comprehensive range of metal commodities to support the region's industrial base.

Middle East

Our Middle Eastern operations focus on supplying metal commodities to the region's growing industrial base and construction sector:

  • Key Markets: Construction, infrastructure, manufacturing
  • Regional Office: Dubai, UAE
  • Logistics Infrastructure: Port facilities, warehousing
  • Client Base: Construction companies, industrial groups, government projects

We serve clients across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, and other Gulf states, providing metals for the region's ambitious construction and infrastructure projects.

Asia

Our Asian distribution network serves the region's dynamic manufacturing sectors, with a focus on:

  • Key Markets: Electronics, automotive, construction, manufacturing
  • Regional Offices: Singapore, Shanghai
  • Logistics Infrastructure: Port facilities, warehousing, distribution centers
  • Client Base: Electronics manufacturers, automotive companies, construction firms

We supply metal commodities to clients in China, Japan, South Korea, and Southeast Asia, supporting the region's rapid industrial growth and technological advancement.

Regional Offices & Representatives

To support our global operations, Swiss Metal Trading maintains strategic regional offices and representatives in key locations worldwide. These local teams ensure close coordination with suppliers, logistics partners, and clients, while providing deep market knowledge and responsive service.
