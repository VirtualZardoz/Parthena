About Swiss Metal Trading

Learn about our company background, organizational structure, and commitment to excellence in the global metal commodities market.

Company Background

Swiss Metal Trading was established in 1995 in Geneva, Switzerland, with a vision to become a leading player in the global metal commodities market. Over the past three decades, we have grown from a small trading operation to a significant international presence in the metal commodities sector.

Founded by a team of industry veterans with extensive experience in metal trading and international commerce, our company was built on the principles of integrity, reliability, and excellence. These core values continue to guide our operations today as we connect metal producers with markets worldwide.

Our strategic location in Geneva, Switzerland—a global hub for commodity trading—has allowed us to develop strong relationships with financial institutions, logistics partners, and industry stakeholders, further strengthening our position in the market.

Our Mission

To facilitate the efficient and responsible flow of metal commodities from producing regions to global markets, creating value for our partners and contributing to sustainable industrial development worldwide.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all our operations.
  • Reliability: We deliver on our commitments to partners and clients.
  • Responsibility: We operate with respect for communities and the environment.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our processes and services.

Organizational Structure

Swiss Metal Trading operates with a streamlined organizational structure designed to maximize efficiency and expertise across our global operations. Our company is organized into specialized divisions that work in close coordination to ensure seamless service delivery.

Executive Leadership

Our executive team brings decades of combined experience in the metal commodities sector, providing strategic direction and oversight to all company operations. Led by our CEO and supported by the Chief Financial Officer, Chief Operations Officer, and Chief Trading Officer, the executive leadership ensures alignment with our company vision and values.

Trading Division

The core of our business, our Trading Division is composed of specialized teams focused on specific metal commodities. Each team is led by experienced traders with deep market knowledge and supported by market analysts who monitor global trends and price movements to inform trading decisions.

Origination Division

Our Origination Division manages relationships with suppliers in metal-producing regions, primarily in Africa and South America. This team works closely with miners, processors, and local partners to secure high-quality metal commodities while ensuring compliance with all relevant regulations and standards.

Distribution Division

The Distribution Division oversees our global network spanning North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. This team manages relationships with end-users, distributors, and industrial clients, ensuring timely delivery and responsive service to meet market demands.

Logistics & Operations

Our Logistics & Operations team coordinates the complex movement of metal commodities across global supply chains. Working with shipping partners, warehousing facilities, and customs authorities, this division ensures efficient and reliable transportation from origin to destination.

Finance & Compliance

The Finance & Compliance division manages financial operations, risk management, and regulatory compliance. This team ensures adherence to international trade regulations, financial reporting standards, and industry best practices across all our global operations.

Geneva Headquarters

Our global headquarters is strategically located in Geneva, Switzerland, a city renowned as a global hub for commodity trading and international commerce. From this central location, we coordinate our worldwide operations and maintain close connections with financial institutions, industry partners, and regulatory bodies.

The Geneva headquarters houses our executive leadership, central trading operations, and key administrative functions. Our modern facilities are designed to foster collaboration and innovation while providing our team with the resources needed to serve our global network effectively.
